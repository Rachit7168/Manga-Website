About One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King. Filled with epic battles, heartfelt friendships, and mysterious lore, One Piece is one of the most iconic manga of all time.
